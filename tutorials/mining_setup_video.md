# Mining Setup Video Tutorial

## Overview
This tutorial will guide you through creating your first mining setup video for RustChain. Completing this bounty will earn you 10 RTC tokens.

## Requirements
- A working RustChain mining setup
- Screen recording software (OBS, Loom, etc.)
- Microphone for commentary
- Basic video editing skills

## Video Requirements
Your video should cover the following topics:

### 1. Introduction (1-2 minutes)
- Brief introduction of yourself
- What motivated you to start mining RustChain
- Overview of what viewers will learn

### 2. Hardware Setup (3-5 minutes)
- Show your mining hardware (CPU, GPU, RAM)
- Explain why you chose this configuration
- Show any cooling solutions

### 3. Software Installation (5-7 minutes)
- Download and install RustChain miner
- Configure mining software
- Set up wallet

### 4. Mining Configuration (3-5 minutes)
- Explain your mining strategy
- Show pool selection (if applicable)
- Demonstrate mining commands

### 5. Monitoring and Optimization (2-3 minutes)
- Show how to monitor mining performance
- Explain any optimization techniques
- Share your hashrate and efficiency

### 6. Troubleshooting (2-3 minutes)
- Common issues you encountered
- How you resolved them
- Tips for avoiding problems

### 7. Conclusion (1 minute)
- Summary of your setup
- Encourage viewers to start mining
- Mention your RustChain address for tips

## Submission Guidelines
1. Upload your video to YouTube or Vimeo
2. Include "RustChain Mining Setup" in the title
3. Add a detailed description with timestamps
4. Submit the video link through the bounty system
5. Include your RustChain wallet address for payment

## Tips for Success
- Speak clearly and at a moderate pace
- Show close-ups of important details
- Include on-screen text for key commands
- Keep the video under 20 minutes
- Add background music if appropriate
- Use good lighting for clear visuals

## Bounty Payment
Once your video is approved by the RustChain team, 10 RTC will be sent to your wallet address within 7 days.
