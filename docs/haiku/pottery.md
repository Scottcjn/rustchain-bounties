# Pottery Haiku Collection

## About
This collection explores pottery and ceramics through haiku poetry. From throwing clay to glazing, celebrate the ancient art of shaping earth.

## Haiku Categories

### Techniques
- wheel throwing
- hand building
- sculpting
- glazing

### Pottery Types
- bowls and plates
- vases and jars
- cups and mugs
- decorative pieces

### The Process
- centering clay
- shaping on wheel
- drying and firing
- final glaze

## Sample Haiku

```
Clay spins on wheel
Hands shape the spinning earth
Vase takes form now
```

```
Fire in the kiln
Transforms soft clay to stone
Art lasts forever
```

```
Glaze drips down slow
Rainbow colors emerge
Beauty from the earth
```
