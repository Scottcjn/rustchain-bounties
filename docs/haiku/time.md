# Time Haiku Collection

## About
Reflect on the nature of time through haiku poetry. Time flows ever forward, yet moments live forever in memory.

## Haiku Categories

### Time Passing
- morning to night
- seasons change
- years go by
- aging gracefully

### Moments
- fleeting moments
- memorable occasions
- everyday moments
- special celebrations

### Time Perception
- waiting and patience
- time flies
- timelessness
- eternity

## Sample Haiku

```
Seconds become minutes
Minutes become hours
Life flows like water
```

```
Child becomes adult
Parents become elders
Time shows its face
```

```
This moment now
Will never come again
Cherish the present
```
