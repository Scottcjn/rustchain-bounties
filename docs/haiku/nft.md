# NFT Haiku Collection

## About
This collection celebrates Non-Fungible Tokens and digital ownership through haiku poetry. From digital art to gaming items, explore the unique world of NFTs.

## Haiku Categories

### Digital Art
- generative art
- 3D sculptures
- pixel art
- animated pieces

### Collectibles
- trading cards
- virtual real estate
- domain names
- profile pictures

### Gaming NFTs
- in-game items
- virtual land
- playable characters
- guild memberships

## Sample Haiku

```
Token unique lives
On the blockchain forever
Ownership proven
```

```
Digital art trades

Now truly mine to keep
Play to earn reward
```
