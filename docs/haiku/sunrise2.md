# Sunrise Haiku Collection

## About
This collection celebrates sunrises and new beginnings through haiku poetry. From dawn breaking to first light, embrace the day.

## Haiku Categories

### Morning Light
- colors in sky
- gradual brightening
- mist and fog
- quiet world

### Experience
- early morning
- mountaintop views
- beach sunrises
- city skylines

### Feelings
- hope
- renewal
- peace
- wonder

## Sample Haiku

```
Dark sky turns pink
Sun rises over the hills
New day begins
```

```
Misty morning
Golden light breaks through clouds
World awakens
```

```
Early runner
First light guides the way
Peace in motion
```
