# DragonFruit Haiku Collection

## About
This collection celebrates dragon fruit and exotic fruits through haiku poetry. From tropical farms to your plate, explore the unique fruit.

## Haiku Categories

### Appearance
- pink skin
- green scales
- white or red flesh
- tiny seeds

### Taste
- mildly sweet
- kiwi-like texture
- refreshing
- subtle floral

### Growing
- cactus family
- tropical climate
- night-blooming flowers
- harvest time

## Sample Haiku

```
Pink dragon fruit
Green scales dot the surface
Beautiful skin
```

```
Cut it open
White flesh with tiny seeds
Refreshing taste
```

```
Cactus flower
Blooms only at night
Morning fruit forms
```
