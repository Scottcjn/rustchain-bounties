# Sunset Haiku Collection

## About
This collection celebrates sunsets and the end of day through haiku poetry. From golden hour to twilight, appreciate the day's end.

## Haiku Categories

### Colors
- orange and pink
- purple and red
- golden hour
- gradient skies

### Settings
- beach sunsets
- mountain sunsets
- city sunsets
- desert sunsets

### Feelings
- reflection
- gratitude
- peace
- beauty

## Sample Haiku

```
Orange and pink
Sun dips below the horizon
Day turns to night
```

```
Golden light fades
Stars begin to appear
Evening arrives
```

```
Silhouette forms
Against the colorful sky
Peaceful end
```
