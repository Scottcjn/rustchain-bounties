# Water Haiku Collection

## About
Explore the many forms and meanings of water through haiku poetry. Water is life, flowing through all existence.

## Haiku Categories

### Water Forms
- rivers and streams
- lakes and ponds
- rain and drizzle
- ocean and sea

### Water States
- calm waters
- rapids and falls
- frozen ice
- rising flood

### Water Activities
- swimming
- fishing
- sailing
- bathing

## Sample Haiku

```
River flows on
Carving paths through ancient stone
Patient strength moves
```

```
Rain falls soft down
Puddles form on city streets
Life drinks deep here
```

```
Morning dew rests
On blades of grass so fine
World washes clean
```

## Themes
- Flow and change
- Life source
- Purification
- Power and calm
- Nature's cycles
