# Animals Haiku Collection

## About
This collection explores the animal kingdom through the lens of haiku poetry. From domestic pets to wild creatures, celebrate the diverse creatures that share our world.

## Haiku Categories

### Wild Animals
- safari creatures
- jungle animals
- ocean marine life
- birds of prey
- nocturnal animals

### Domestic Animals
- dogs and puppies
- cats and kittens
- farm animals
- companion pets

### Insects & Arthropods
- butterflies
- bees and wasps
- beetles
- spiders

## Sample Haiku

```
Lion rests in shade
Golden mane catches sunlight
Savanna calls home
```

```
Butterfly lands
On a summer flower
Colors dance together
```

```

Wagging tail, loving eyes
Friendship unconditional
```
