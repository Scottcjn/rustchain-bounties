# Golf Haiku Collection

## About
This collection celebrates golf and the sport of precision through haiku poetry. From fairways to greens, embrace the gentlemanly game.

## Haiku Categories

### The Game

- par-3 holes
- par-4 and par-5
- putting greens

### Equipment
- drivers and woods
- irons and wedges
- putters
- golf bags

### The Experience
- early morning tee times
- walk the course
- clubhouse stories
- handicap tracking

## Sample Haiku

```
Club swings with grace
Ball flies in an arc
Landing on the green
```

```
Fourteen clubs in bag
Strategy on each hole
Patience wins the game
```

```
Caddie offers advice
Wind and distance calculated
Birdie within reach
```
