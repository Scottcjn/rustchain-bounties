## Sample Haiku

```
Dough rises slow
Patience yields perfect crumb
Air fills the loaf
```

```
Score the top quick
Oven door shuts tight
Crust will form now
```

```
Bread comes out golden
Aroma fills the kitchen
Warm and delicious
```
