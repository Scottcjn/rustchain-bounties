# Seasons Haiku Collection

## About
Celebrate the four seasons through haiku poetry. Each season brings its own beauty, moods, and reflections.

## Haiku Categories

### Spring
- cherry blossoms
- spring rain
- new beginnings
- blooming flowers

### Summer
- beach and sun
- summer storms
- long days
- tropical heat

### Autumn/Fall
- falling leaves
- harvest moon
- autumn colors
- cooling temperatures

### Winter
- snow scenes
- winter solstice
- frost and ice
- cozy interiors

## Sample Haiku

```
Cherry blossoms fall
Petals paint the pathway
Spring arrives soft
```

```
Leaves turn gold and red
Autumn paints the landscape
Harvest time is here
```

```
Snow covers ground
World sleeps in white blanket
Winter whispers peace
```
