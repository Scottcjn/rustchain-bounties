# Sky Haiku Collection

## About
Gaze upward at the ever-changing sky through haiku poetry. The sky holds our dreams, our weather, our wonder.

## Haiku Categories

### Sky States
- clear blue sky
- cloudy days
- starry nights
- sunrise and sunset

### Weather in Sky
- rain clouds
- thunderstorms
- snow clouds
- rainbow appearances

### Celestial
- sun and moon
- stars and planets
- eclipses
- meteor showers

## Sample Haiku

```
Blue stretches wide
Clouds drift like cotton balls
Peace in the heavens
```

```
Stars emerge now
Twinkling ancient light reaches
Eyes that see tonight
```

```
Sunset paints sky
Orange blends to purple deep
Day bids farewell soft
```
