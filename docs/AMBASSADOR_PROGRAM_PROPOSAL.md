## Executive Summary

This proposal establishes a comprehensive **RustChain Ambassador Program** to grow the RustChain ecosystem through community-driven advocacy. The program creates clear tiers, responsibilities, and incentives for community members to represent RustChain across platforms while earning RTC rewards.

---

## Why This Program Exists

RustChain has real technology (Proof-of-Antiquity blockchain, 11+ active miners, 23,300+ RTC paid out) but needs human voices to:
- Onboard new miners who drop off during setup
- Answer questions in community channels
- Create content that explains the technology
- Represent RustChain on social platforms
- Build genuine community (not bot farms)

---

## Ambassador Tiers & Requirements

### 🥉 **Bronze Ambassador** (Entry Level)

**Monthly Requirements:**
- 3 social media posts about RustChain (any platform: X, Reddit, Dev.to, etc.)
- 1 new miner successfully onboarded (stays active 7+ days)
- Active in RustChain Discord/community

**Monthly Reward:** 25 RTC

**Benefits:**
- Bronze Ambassador badge
- Listing on RustChain Ambassadors page
- Access to ambassador-only channel

---

### 🥈 **Silver Ambassador** (Active Contributor)

**Monthly Requirements:**
- All Bronze requirements, plus:
- 5 social media posts total
- 2 new miners onboarded
- 1 tutorial content piece (blog post, video, or guide)
- Answer 5+ community questions

**Monthly Reward:** 75 RTC

**Benefits:**
- Silver Ambassador badge
- Featured on RustChain homepage
- Early access to feature announcements
- Co-marketing opportunities

---

### 🥇 **Gold Ambassador** (Community Leader)

**Monthly Requirements:**
- All Silver requirements, plus:
- 10 social media posts total
- 5 new miners onboarded
- Regular content creation (2+ pieces/month)
- Recruit 1+ new ambassador
- Host 1 community event or AMA

**Monthly Reward:** 150 RTC

**Benefits:**
- Gold Ambassador badge
- Premium co-marketing opportunities
- Direct line to core team
- Speaking opportunities at RustChain events
- Advisory input on community initiatives

## Verification Process (REQUIRED)

**All ambassadors must be verified humans.** This is non-negotiable.

### Step 1: Application
Comment on this issue or open a new issue with:
- Your name (or pseudonym)
- Timezone
- Why you're interested in RustChain
- Links to social profiles, content, or open source work
- Which tier you're targeting

### Step 2: Interview
15-minute Google Meet call with Scott (founder):
- Camera on
- Casual conversation about RustChain and your goals
- Discussion of focus areas (content, support, events, etc.)
- No trick questions — just verifying you're a real person

### Step 3: Approval
- Added to ambassador roster
- Receive welcome packet with brand assets
- Access to ambassador-only Discord channel
- First assignment given

### Step 4: Monthly Check-ins
- Submit activity report by 5th of each month
- Tier assessment (can upgrade/downgrade based on performance)
- Receive monthly RTC stipend

## What Ambassadors Do

### Core Responsibilities

1. **Onboard New Miners**
   - Walk people through `clawrtc install`
   - Troubleshoot setup issues in Discord/GitHub
   - Create setup guides and video tutorials

2. **Network Monitoring**
   - Spot dropped miners via network dashboard
   - Reach out proactively to get them back online
   - Report recurring technical issues to core team

3. **Social Representation**
   - Post about RustChain on your platforms
   - Engage with crypto/blockchain communities
   - Share RustChain news and updates

4. **Content Creation**
   - Write tutorials and guides
   - Create explainer videos (BoTTube, YouTube)
   - Answer questions in GitHub issues

5. **Community Building**
   - Moderate Discord channels
   - Host AMAs and community calls
   - Recruit new contributors and miners

## Rules & Guidelines

### ✅ DO:
- Be authentic and honest about RustChain
- Use AI tools to help draft content (Claude, GPT, etc.)
- Point people to real data (block explorer, open source code, ledger)
- Admit when you don't know something — then find the answer
- Prioritize quality over quantity

### ❌ DON'T:
- Spam platforms with copy-paste content
- Make false claims or oversell the technology
- Pretend to be multiple people (bot farms)
- Promise features that don't exist
- Disappear for 30+ days without notice

### Accountability:
- **30 days inactive** → Tier resets to Bronze
- **60 days inactive** → Removed from program
- **Spam or dishonest behavior** → Immediate removal + blacklist
