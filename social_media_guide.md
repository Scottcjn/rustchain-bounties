# Social Media Guide for RustChain Promotion

## Steps to Fulfill the Requirements:

1. **Choose a Platform:**
   - X (Twitter)
   - Reddit
   - Hacker News

2. **Create the Post:**
   - Write a post in your own words about RustChain and Proof of Antiquity.
   - Include a link to the GitHub repository: https://github.com/Scottcjn/Rustchain.
   - Ensure the post is genuine and follows the rules of the platform.

3. **Publish the Post:**
   - Publish the post on the chosen platform.
   - Make sure the post stays live for at least 24 hours.

4. **Gather Proof:**
   - Take a screenshot of the post showing the timestamp and your account.
   - Keep the direct link to the post.

5. **Claim the Payout:**
   - After the post has been live for 24 hours, comment on the GitHub issue with:
     1. The link to the post.
     2. The screenshot.
     3. Your RTC wallet address.

```
GitHub: https://github.com/Scottcjn/Rustchain
#blockchain #rustlang #crypto
```
