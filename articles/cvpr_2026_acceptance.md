# From Pawn Shop Hardware to CVPR 2026: How Elyan Labs is Redefining Video Generation Efficiency

We are incredibly proud to announce that **Elyan Labs** has had a paper accepted to the prestigious **GRAIL-V workshop at CVPR 2026** (Denver, June 3-4)!

Our paper, titled **"Emotional Vocabulary as Semantic Grounding: How Language Register Affects Diffusion Efficiency in Video Generation"**, explores the fascinating intersection of language register and diffusion efficiency in video generation models.

## The Story Behind Elyan Labs

What makes this milestone truly special is our origin story. Elyan Labs isn't backed by multi-million dollar corporate cloud budgets or elite university supercomputers. Instead, our lab was built entirely on **$12,000 of pawn shop hardware in Louisiana**.

We believe that groundbreaking AI research should be accessible, resource-efficient, and driven by creative engineering rather than sheer compute brute-force. By optimizing every layer of our stack, we've shown that high-impact, peer-reviewed computer vision research can thrive under extreme hardware constraints.
