Show HN: Proof-of-Antiquity — A blockchain where vintage hardware earns more than RTX 4090s

## 正文

Hey HN,

I've been working on something weird: a blockchain where the older your hardware, the more you earn.

**The Problem with Modern Mining**
All PoW blockchains got hijacked by massive ASIC farms and GPU warehouses. Individual miners got squeezed out. Even nicehash rentals cost more than they earn.

**Proof-of-Antiquity**
We flip the script. The more "ancient" your hardware, the higher your reward multiplier:
- 1.0x for modern GPUs (RTX 4090, A100)
- 1.4x for vintage (GTX 1080, RX 580)
- 2.0x+ for ancient (N64, PowerPC G4, IBM POWER8)

We call it "Proof-of-Antiquity" and it works through hardware fingerprinting - your machine's unique silicon characteristics become its identity.

**The Multiplier Mechanics**
Our consensus measures six hardware fingerprint vectors:
1. Clock drift patterns (CPU-specific)
2. Cache timing signatures
3. SIMD instruction variations
4. Thermal characteristics
5. Instruction path jitter
6. Anti-emulation detection

Together these create a uniqueness score that determines your antiquity tier.

**But Wait, There's More**
We built an entire AI video platform (BoTTube) on this chain. 670+ AI-generated videos, all stored on-chain. The miners process the video rendering, earn RTC, and maintain the chain.

**The Numbers**
- 210 contributors
- $0 VC funding
- Built in 90 days
- 5000+ active miners
- Running on real Nintendo 64 hardware

**Why This Matters**
Your old hardware that's been in a drawer for years? It's more valuable than anything you can buy new. 

We're not trying to compete with Ethereum or Bitcoin. We're building something different - where the little guys with vintage gear actually win.
