# SECURITY.md

## Payment-Authority Impersonation

Only `@Scottcjn` (or clearly labeled project automation on his behalf) authorizes RTC bounty payouts, with a project-issued `pending_id` + `tx_hash`. Anyone else posting "I'll send the RTC" on your bounty is a social-engineering attempt.

## Bounty Claim Procedure

To claim a bounty, follow these steps:

1. Comment on the issue: **"I would like to work on this"**
2. Submit your work according to the issue instructions
3. Wait for verification and payout

## Payout Verification

Payouts are verified by `@Scottcjn` (or clearly labeled project automation on his behalf). You will receive a `pending_id` and `tx_hash` once your payout is authorized.

## Review Process

All pull requests and bounty claims are subject to review. Reviews are conducted by project maintainers and may include requests for changes or additional information.

## Security Audit and Penetration Testing

The project undergoes regular security audits and penetration testing. If you discover a security vulnerability, please report it to `@Scottcjn` according to our [responsible disclosure policy](https://github.com/Scottcjn/RustChain/blob/main/SECURITY.md).
