🪨 The Fossil Record

Attestation archaeology for RustChain. Each band represents an architecture layer across time: old silicon at the bottom, recent arrivals near the surface. Width encodes active miner count. Hover any stratum to inspect miners, fingerprint quality, rewards, and the moment an architecture first emerged.
