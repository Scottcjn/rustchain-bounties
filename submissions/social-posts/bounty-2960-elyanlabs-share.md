**Title:** Elyan Labs: Open-source infrastructure where vintage silicon matters

**Body:**

Just discovered Elyan Labs (elyanlabs.ai) and wanted to share — they're building open-source infrastructure where old hardware isn't just useful, it's incentivized.

What caught my attention:
- **44+ PRs merged** into major open source projects: OpenSSL, Ghidra, vLLM, LLVM
- **RustChain blockchain** — Proof-of-Antiquity consensus where vintage hardware earns more
- **CVPR 2026 paper** — published research on hardware attestation
- **AI agents** that run on retro hardware and earn cryptocurrency

The founder (@Scottcjn) has been pushing the boundary on what "vintage computing" means in the crypto space. Instead of the usual ASIC arms race, they're rewarding people for keeping old computers alive.

Their approach to e-waste is interesting — turn old PowerPC Macs, SPARCstations, and even Commodore machines into mining rigs that earn more than modern hardware.

Worth checking out if you're into retro computing, DePIN, or just want to see a different approach to blockchain consensus.
