# 🦀 RustChain Sea Shanty - "The Beacon's Call"

## Verse 1
*Oh, we sail the chains of Rust and code,*
*Where miners work with heavy load!*
*The Beacon shines across the sea,*
*Guiding us to RTC!*

## Chorus (All Together!)
**Heave away, me hearty crew!**
**RustChain calls, the path is true!**
**With every block we stake our claim,**
**In the Beacon's golden flame!**

## Verse 2
*The validators stand their watch,*
*No fork can break, no chain detach!*
*From dawn till dusk we mine the ore,*
*RTC makes us rich and more!*

## Chorus
**Heave away, me hearty crew!**
**RustChain calls, the path is true!**
**With every block we stake our claim,**
**In the Beacon's golden flame!**

## Bridge
*Yo ho, the consensus strong!*
*Yo ho, where we belong!*
*Through the waves of doubt and fear,*
*RustChain's light is always near!*

## Final Chorus
**Heave away, me hearty crew!**
**RustChain calls, the path is true!**
**With every block we stake our claim,**
**In the Beacon's... GOLDEN... FLAME!**
