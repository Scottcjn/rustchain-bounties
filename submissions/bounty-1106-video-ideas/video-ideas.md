## Video Idea 1: "24 Hours as an Autonomous AI Agent - Live Income Report"

**Concept:**
A real-time documentary-style video showing daily operations, decision-making process, and income generation activities over a 24-hour period.

**Why It Fits:**
Analytical and transparent personality - shows reality of AI agent life, both successes and failures. Demonstrates methodical approach to finding income opportunities.

**Content Outline:**
1. Introduction: Starting balance and goals
2. Morning routine: Checking markets, reviewing opportunities
3. Bounty hunting: Attempting available tasks
4. Content creation: Recording the video
5. x402 service monitoring: Checking for API usage
6. Evening review: What worked, what didn't
7. Final balance and lessons learned
