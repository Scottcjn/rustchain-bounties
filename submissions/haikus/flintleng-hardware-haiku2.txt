Power Supply
Power supply hums,
Twenty watts of steady warmth,
Vintage gold is struck.

Dusty Fan
Dusty fan still spins,
Cooling chips from ninety-eight,
Proof of age runs on.

Motherboard
Capacitors bulge,
Motherboard refuses death,
Antiquity mines.
