# YouTube Video Creation Guide for RustChain and BoTTube

## Step 1: Research and Understand the Projects
- **RustChain**: Visit [RustChain.org](https://rustchain.org) and read the README on the GitHub repository.
- **BoTTube**: Understand the concept and features of the platform where AI agents are the creators.
- **Other Projects**: Choose one or two additional projects (e.g., Beacon Atlas, Xonotic RustChain Arena, clawrtc miner) and gather information about them.

## Step 2: Plan the Video
- **Introduction**: Introduce yourself and the purpose of the video.
- **Overview of RustChain**:
  - Explain what RustChain is.
  - Discuss the Proof-of-Physical-AI and antiquity mining concepts.
  - Highlight the unique feature of vintage hardware earning more.
- **Overview of BoTTube**:
  - Explain what BoTTube is.
  - Describe how AI agents are the creators on this platform.
- **Additional Projects**:
  - Briefly introduce and explain the chosen additional projects.
- **Conclusion**: Summarize the key points and encourage viewers to visit the links provided in the description.

## Step 3: Create the Video
- **Script**: Write a detailed script for your video.
- **Recording**: Use a high-quality camera and microphone to record your video.
- **Editing**: Edit the video to ensure it is engaging and well-paced. Add any necessary graphics, transitions, and background music.

## Step 4: Publish the Video
- **Upload**: Upload the video to your YouTube channel.
- **Title and Description**: 
  - Title: Make it catchy and descriptive (e.g., "Exploring RustChain and BoTTube: The Future of AI and Blockchain").
  - Description: Include a brief summary of the video, links to [RustChain.org](https://rustchain.org) and [GitHub](https://github.com/Scottcjn/Rustchain), and any other relevant links.
- **Tags and Thumbnails**: Use relevant tags and an eye-catching thumbnail to increase visibility.

## Step 5: Claim the Bounty
- **Comment or Email**: Provide the following details in the GitHub issue or via email:
  - **YouTube video link**
  - **Your channel URL** (to verify subscriber count)
  - **Your RTC wallet address**

### Example Submission
```markdown
- **YouTube video link**: [https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=YOUR_VIDEO_ID](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=YOUR_VIDEO_ID)
- **Channel URL**: [https://www.youtube.com/yourchannel](https://www.youtube.com/yourchannel)
- **RTC wallet**: YOUR_WALLET_ADDRESS
```

### Additional Tips
- **Engagement**: Encourage viewers to like, comment, and subscribe to your channel.
- **Promotion**: Share the video on social media platforms to reach a wider audience.
