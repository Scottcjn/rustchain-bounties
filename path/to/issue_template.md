## Bounty Claim Process

To claim the bounty for starring and sharing `beacon-skill`, please follow these steps:

1. **Star the Repository**: Visit [beacon-skill on GitHub](https://github.com/Scottcjn/beacon-skill) and star it.

2. **Share on Social Media**: Post about the `beacon-skill` repository on a social platform of your choice. Ensure your post includes:
   - A link to the repository
   - A brief description of what it does

3. **Submit Proof**: Provide a screenshot of your star on GitHub and a link to your social media post as proof of your claim.

We will verify your submission and transfer the RTC to your wallet. Thank you for your support!
